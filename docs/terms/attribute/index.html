Attribute

Short Description#

An Attribute is data, that represents a characteristic that a party (the owner of the attribute) has attributed to an entity. An attribute is a form of assertion (or claim), that states the attribution of that characteristic to its subject.

Related topics can be found in the identity pattern, the identification pattern and the identifier pattern.
